Waystones is available under a commercial license for organizations that cannot comply with the terms of the [GNU Affero General Public License v3](LICENSE).

## When do you need a commercial license?

You need a commercial license if you:

- Embed or integrate Waystones into a proprietary product or SaaS offering
- Distribute Waystones as part of a commercial product without releasing your source code under AGPL v3
- Otherwise cannot meet the copyleft obligations of AGPL v3

## How to obtain a commercial license

[Open an issue](../../issues) in this repository with the subject **"Commercial License Inquiry"** and describe your use case. We will get back to you with pricing and terms.

## Open source use

If your use case is compatible with the AGPL v3, you are free to use Waystones at no cost under those terms. See [LICENSE](LICENSE) for details.
